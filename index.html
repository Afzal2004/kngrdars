About Us

A Legacy of Learning, A Future of Guidance

Muhyissunna Dars Kannangar was founded to nurture generations of students in Islamic theology, ethics, and community leadership, blending time-honoured scholarship with the needs of a modern world.

Our mission is to raise sincere scholars and grounded individuals — students who carry knowledge with humility and serve their communities with compassion and integrity.

Our Vision

A community enriched by scholars who embody the Sunnah in thought, character and action.

Our Values

Sincerity, discipline, brotherhood and lifelong pursuit of sacred knowledge.

Education

A structured curriculum spanning Fiqh, Aqeedah, Hadith, Arabic and Tazkiyah.

Community

Outreach programs, guidance circles and services that extend beyond the classroom.
