Memories & Journey

A Timeline of Growth

Year 1 — Admission

Joining the Dars

Students begin their journey with foundational Arabic and Islamic studies, guided by senior scholars.

Year 3 — Milestone

Specialised Studies Begin

Students choose focus areas in Fiqh, Hadith, or Tafsir, deepening their scholarship under mentorship.

Year 6 — Graduation

Dars Completion

Graduates are honoured in a formal ceremony, ready to serve their communities with confidence and sincerity.

Beyond — Legacy

Continuing the Journey

Alumni go on to lead mosques, schools, businesses and community organisations across the globe.
